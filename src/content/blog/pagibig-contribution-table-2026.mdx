# Pag-IBIG Contribution Table (2026 Update)

The Home Development Mutual Fund (HDMF), more commonly known as the Pag-IBIG Fund, recently implemented its first major rate increase since 1986. Understanding the new mandatory contribution rates is essential for both employees and employers.

## The New Maximum Fund Salary (MFS)

For decades, the Maximum Fund Salary (MFS) used to compute Pag-IBIG contributions was capped at ₱5,000. Under the new guidelines, the MFS has been doubled to **₱10,000**. 

Because the contribution rate remains at 2%, this means the maximum monthly contribution has doubled.

## The 2026 Pag-IBIG Contribution Rates

The contribution is shared equally between the employee and the employer.

### For Employees Earning ₱1,500 and below:
- **Employee Share**: 1%
- **Employer Share**: 2%

### For Employees Earning over ₱1,500 (Up to ₱10,000 cap):
- **Employee Share**: 2%
- **Employer Share**: 2%

## How It Affects Your Salary

If you earn exactly or more than ₱10,000 a month:
- **Employee Deduction**: ₱10,000 x 2% = **₱200**
- **Employer Contribution**: ₱10,000 x 2% = **₱200**
- **Total Monthly Savings**: **₱400**

Previously, the maximum deduction was only ₱100. This increase means your take-home pay decreases by an additional ₱100, but your total forced savings inside Pag-IBIG grows twice as fast.

## Voluntary Members, OFWs, and Freelancers

If you are self-employed, an Overseas Filipino Worker (OFW), or a voluntary paying member, there is no employer to cover the other half. Therefore, you must shoulder both the employee and employer shares.

- **Total Monthly Contribution**: 4% of your declared income (Up to the ₱10,000 cap).
- **Maximum Voluntary Payment**: **₱400 per month**.

## Why the Increase is a Good Thing

While nobody likes higher deductions, your Pag-IBIG Regular Savings account earns annual dividends entirely tax-free. Historically, Pag-IBIG dividends hover between 5.5% to 6.5%. By saving ₱400 a month instead of ₱200, your retirement fund and eligibility for massive housing loans increase significantly.
