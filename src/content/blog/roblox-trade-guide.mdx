# Roblox Trading Fairness Guide

In major Roblox games like *Adopt Me!* and *Blox Fruits*, trading is a central mechanic that allows players to collect rare pets, items, and fruits. However, because value scales are unofficial and shift quickly, players often worry about getting scammed or overpaying. 

Our point-based **Roblox Trading Fairness Calculator** helps you estimate trade ratios, ensuring you secure a Win (W) or a Fair Trade, and avoid a painful Loss (L).

## The Point-Based Fairness System Explained

To standardize values across various tiers (such as a Frost Dragon vs. Ultra-Rare neon pets), trading communities use generalized point weights:
- **Common items:** 1 point
- **Uncommon items:** 3 points
- **Rare items:** 10 points
- **Ultra-Rare items:** 30 points
- **Legendary items:** 100 points
- **Mythical/High-Tier items:** 500 points

By adding up the scores on both your side and the other player's side, you can instantly see if the point totals are within a balanced **10% threshold** (the industry standard for a "Fair Trade").

## How to Trade Safely on Roblox

1. **Double Check Trades:** Always use our fairness calculator to check the point values before pressing the final accept button.
2. **Beware of Trust Trades:** Never agree to "trust trades" or multi-part trades where you are asked to give items first with the promise of receiving items later.
3. **Verify Neon and Mega Variants:** Neon pets generally hold 4x the value of their standard counterparts, while Mega Neons hold 16x the value. Make sure you select the correct rarity weight.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What makes a trade a Win (W) for me?
A trade is considered a "Win" (W) if the items you are receiving hold a point value at least 10% higher than the items you are offering.

### What should I do if the calculator says it's a Loss (L)?
If you are overpaying, ask the other trader to add supplementary uncommon/rare items, or remove one of your higher-tier items until the points line up under a "Fair Trade" (🤝) status.
