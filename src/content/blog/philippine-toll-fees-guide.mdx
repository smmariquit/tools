Toll expressways in the Philippines can save you hours of travel time, but the fees add up quickly. If you're planning a trip from Manila to Baguio, Clark, or Tagaytay, knowing the exact toll fees can help you budget your road trip properly.

## Major Expressways and Their Operators

*   **NLEX (North Luzon Expressway):** Operated by NLEX Corporation. Connects Manila to Pampanga/Tarlac.
*   **SLEX (South Luzon Expressway):** Operated by South Luzon Tollway Corporation. Connects Manila to Laguna/Batangas.
*   **TPLEX (Tarlac-Pangasinan-La Union Expressway):** Connects Tarlac to Rosario, La Union.
*   **Skyway System (Stages 1-3):** The elevated expressway system connecting Makati to Alabang (Stage 1), and NLEX to SLEX (Stage 3).
*   **CALAX (Cavite-Laguna Expressway):** Connects Cavite to Laguna via SLEX.

## Autosweep vs Easytrip

The Philippines has two RFID systems:
*   **Autosweep:** Used on SLEX, Skyway, NAIA Expressway, and CALAX.
*   **Easytrip:** Used on NLEX, SCTEX, CAVITEX, and C5 Link.

Make sure you have both RFID stickers if you frequently travel between North and South Luzon!
