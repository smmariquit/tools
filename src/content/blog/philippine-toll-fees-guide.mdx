## Autosweep vs Easytrip RFID Guide

To travel seamlessly across Luzon expressways, you need the right RFID tags:

1. **Autosweep RFID:**
   - **Covered Expressways:** SLEX, Skyway System (Stage 1-3), NAIAX, STAR Tollway, MCX, TPLEX.
   - **Operator:** San Miguel Corporation (SMC) Infrastructure.

2. **Easytrip RFID:**
   - **Covered Expressways:** NLEX, SCTEX, CAVITEX, CALAX, C5 Link.
   - **Operator:** Metro Pacific Tollways Corporation (MPTC).

*Tip: Make sure both RFID tags are installed and sufficiently loaded to avoid blocking toll lanes and incurring penalties!*
