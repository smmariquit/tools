Web3 gaming is a major industry in the Philippines, with millions of players earning digital assets in games like *Pixels* and *Axie Infinity*. ## The Off-Ramping Journey: From Tokens to GCash

To convert tokens like SLP, RON, or PIXEL into PHP, players typically follow these stages:
1. **In-game Claiming:** Claiming tokens from the game contract into your Ronin or Web3 Wallet (requires minor gas fees).
2. **Transferring to Exchange:** Depositing tokens to an exchange like Binance or Coins.ph.
3. **P2P Trading:** Selling your cryptocurrency on P2P (Peer-to-Peer) markets directly to buyers who pay you in GCash, Maya, or via bank transfer.

## Essential Web3 Off-Ramp Cost Factors

When calculating your actual take-home money, make sure to factor in these three critical cost centers:

### 1. Blockchain Gas Fees
Every transaction on the blockchain requires network gas. On the Ronin network, transaction fees are paid in **RON** and are extremely low (typically less than 0.01 RON per transaction). However, you must always maintain a small balance of RON in your wallet to cover gas.

### 2. Binance P2P Spread
On Peer-to-Peer markets, merchants set their own bid and ask rates. The P2P exchange rate is often slightly different from the spot exchange rate. This difference is known as the **spread** and typically represents between **1.0% and 2.0%** of your total trade value.

### 3. Spot Slippage
If you trade large quantities of tokens, swapping them for USD stablecoins (USDT) before cashing out via P2P can incur minor price slippage, which affects your ultimate payout.
